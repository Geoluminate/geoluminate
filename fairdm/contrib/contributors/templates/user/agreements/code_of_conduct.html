1. Respectful and Professional Communication:
  • Treat all users with respect, professionalism, and courtesy.
  • Use inclusive and appropriate language when interacting with others.
  • Avoid personal attacks, harassment, or discriminatory behavior.
  • Refrain from engaging in any form of bullying or intimidation.
2. Protection of Intellectual Property:
  • Respect copyright laws and intellectual property rights.
  • Do not upload or share any content that infringes upon the intellectual property rights of others without proper authorization or permission.
  • Give appropriate credit and citations when referencing or using the work of others.
3. Data Privacy and Security:
  • Safeguard the privacy and security of research data.
  • Only share data that you have the rights to share or that is intended for public dissemination.
  • Obtain necessary permissions and approvals before sharing sensitive or confidential data.
  • Adhere to any applicable data protection regulations, such as GDPR or HIPAA.
4. Responsible Data Usage:
  • Use research data shared by others responsibly and ethically.
  • Respect any data usage restrictions or licenses associated with shared datasets.
  • Do not misuse or misrepresent data for personal gain or to advance any unethical or illegal activities.
  • Be mindful of the potential biases, limitations, and ethical considerations associated with research data.
5. Collaboration and Cooperation:
  • Foster a collaborative and supportive environment within the research community.
  • Encourage open dialogue, knowledge sharing, and constructive feedback.
  • Be willing to collaborate with others and contribute to the collective growth of the community.
  • Respect the ideas, opinions, and contributions of fellow researchers.
6. Compliance with Applicable Laws and Regulations:
  • Abide by all relevant laws, regulations, and ethical guidelines related to research and data sharing.
  • Ensure compliance with institutional policies, funding agency requirements, and any applicable legal frameworks.
7. Reporting Violations:
  • Report any violations of this code of conduct or any suspicious or unethical behavior to the appropriate administrators or moderators.
  • Provide detailed information and evidence when reporting incidents, while maintaining confidentiality and privacy.
8. Consequences of Violations:
  • Violations of this code of conduct may result in appropriate actions, including warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans from the web application.
  • The severity and consequences of violations will be assessed on a case-by-case basis, taking into account the nature and impact of the violation.

Remember that this code of conduct aims to create a positive and inclusive research community. By adhering to these guidelines, we can foster an environment that promotes collaboration, integrity, and the responsible use of research data.
